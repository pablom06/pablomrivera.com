About Pablo M. Rivera

Pablo M. Rivera is a bilingual operations executive and technologist with over 25 years of experience driving results across construction, property management, field services, and technology sectors. His career spans managing 120+ technicians across 12 U.S. states, deploying Salesforce across multiple markets, and leading digital transformation initiatives that bridge the gap between operational excellence and modern technology.

With a foundation in economics from Yale University and advanced training in full-stack development, data analytics, and Lean Six Sigma, Pablo brings a rare combination of strategic vision and hands-on technical capability. He has led organizations through complex restructurings, built national operations from the ground up, and consistently delivered measurable improvements in efficiency, cost reduction, and team performance.

Pablo is fluent in English and Spanish, enabling him to lead diverse, cross-cultural teams across domestic and international markets. He holds Google certifications in Data Analytics and UX Design, along with professional credentials in Project Management and Salesforce administration.
